Available in 7 on-trend comfort and ethnic flavors

  • 3 breakfast flavors that taste better than any morning wake-up call
  • 4 hearty, filling meal and snack flavors that satisfy any appetite

Breakfast

  • Spinach, Cheese & Egg White

    Egg whites with tender spinach and a fine blend of Swiss and Parmesan cheeses, with a hint of garlic served inside a crispy, flaky dough.

  • Chorizo & Egg

    Pork sausage, scrambled eggs, corn, chile peppers, potatoes and a blend of mozzarella and cheddar cheeses in a flaky crust.

  • Bacon, Egg & Cheese

    Bacon and eggs with a cheddar cheese sauce in an enticing crust.

Snack/Lunch

  • Chicken Tomatillo

    Shredded chicken simmered with tomatillos, chiles, jalapeños, garlic and a hint of lime in a croissant crust.

  • Chipotle Beef

    Tender, slow-cooked beef, potatoes, onions and red bell and poblano peppers with smoky chipotle tomato sauce in a croissant crust.

  • BBQ Recipe Chicken

    A unique southern cornbread crust filled with chicken and corn in a smoky BBQ sauce.

  • Chicken Pot Pie

    Hearty chicken, russet potatoes, peas, carrots, onions and savory sauces all wrapped up into a fluffy biscuit crust.
